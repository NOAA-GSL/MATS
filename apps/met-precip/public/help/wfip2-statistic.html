Statistic

The statistic selector compares one data source to another and generates a plot based on the comparison. Once the desired statistic is selected another selector will appear labelled 'truth data source'. For the sake of the statistic caluclation the 'data source' is considered the experimental value and the 'truth data source' is considered the theoretical value.

Note that the options for both 'data source' and 'truth data source' are the same. The 'truth data source' is named solely for the purpose of statistic calculation and is not more accurate.
