Variable Box:

For certain models, additional variables will be listed in this drop down menu that match what are seen in the discriminator box below. These are two dimensional variables and cannot be verified against observations. Only raw data plots are available at the present time. See the discriminator help box for further details.

Discriminator Box:

Discriminators are two dimensional variables derived from specific models and are used as a filtering option on the verification data produced by this interface. For example, the user can see a model’s wind speed skill when the surface heat flux is inside specific bounds by setting upper and lower thresholds of the discriminator (in this case surface heat flux).

Note: When a discriminator is selected, the upper and lower thresholds are set to the corresponding max and min values possible for that field by default, meaning no filtering will be done.

Currently 15 discriminators are available:

  • sfc_Ri - Richardson Number
  • sfc_hflx - Surface Heat Flux
  • lat_hflx - Latent Heat Flux
  • fric_v - Friction Velocity
  • cdca - Cloud amount
  • ws_10 - Wind Speed at 10 m AGL
  • ws_80 - Wind Speed at 80 m AGL
  • wd_80 - Wind Direction at 80 m AGL
  • hpbl - Planetary Boundary Layer Height
  • u200_10 - East-West wind shear from 200 m to 10 m AGL
  • u50_10 - East-West wind shear from 50 m to 10 m AGL
  • u200_50 - East-West wind shear from 200 m to 50 m AGL
  • t200_0 - Temperature lapse rate from the surface to 200 m AGL
  • dswr - Downward Shortwave Radiation
